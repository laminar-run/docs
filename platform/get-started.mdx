Welcome to Laminar! This guide will help you get started with the platform, and will walk you through the process of creating and running your first Flow.

## Create an account

As a first step, you'll need to create an account on Laminar.

<CardGroup>
    <Card
        title="Sign up/Sign in"
        href="https://app.laminar.run/auth/signin?ref=docs"
        icon="user"
    >
        Create or sign in to your Laminar account.
    </Card>
</CardGroup>

This will take you to the Laminar app, where you can create an account. Once you've created an account, you'll be able to create your first Workspace.
