![Demo](https://github.com/laminar-run/public-assets/blob/main/Create%20A%20Flow.gif?raw=true)

Now that you have a Workspace, you can create a Flow.

1. From your Workspace Dashboard, click **Create Flow** on the bottom left of the Flows table.
2. Give your Flow a name and a description and click **Save**.

You'll be able to edit and test your new Flow in the Flow Editor.
